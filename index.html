Who we are

Excellence International Schools is an Islamic educational community dedicated to providing high-quality learning in an environment rooted in faith, discipline, and virtue.

With campuses in Côte d’Ivoire and Nigeria, we serve families who share a vision of excellence in both academic and spiritual development. Our schools build strong partnerships between students, parents, and the community to nurture well-rounded learners and responsible global citizens.

Graduates of Excellence International Schools are well-prepared to face the challenges of higher education, grounded in Islamic faith, moral integrity, and lifelong values . Guided by our commitment to excellence and continuous improvement, our institutions operate under recognized international educational standards.
